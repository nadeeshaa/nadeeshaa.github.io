Nadeesha Athukorala

I am a temporary instructor at the Department of Molecular Biology and Biotechnology, University of Peradeniya.

I graduated with a BS in Molecular Biology and Biotechnology from the University of Peradeniya in 2021 along with first class honours and the university award for academic excellence.

I did my thesis on "Identification of a lipolytic Trichoderma sp. and characterization of its crude extracellular lipase" under the supervision of Prof. Preminda Samaraweera.

I am interested in virology, immunology, developmental biology, reproductive biology, oncology, and biochemistry. Has an interest in exploring novel biocatalysts with industrially demanding properties (especially the medical importance of biocatalysts).
I am seeking PhD/MS oppurtunities in the field of Molecular Biology for 2022 Fall.
